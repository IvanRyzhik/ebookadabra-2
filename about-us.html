About us

Ebookadabra is a reading experience aimed at young families with kids aged from three to seven years old. It is a world where kids will discover books for themselves, be rewarded for their reading and be able to share the experience with their family. We offer a family hundreds of books and related learning activities on a subscription business model whereby a subscriber is granted unlimited access in return for payment of a fee.

Ebookadabra is operated by Moondog Books Limited, a company registered in England and Wales with number 08960113 and registered offices at Lynton House, 7-12 Tavistock Square, London, WC1H 9LT, United Kingdom ("Moondog Books"). The copyright in the book content and book-derived images that we present in Ebookadabra is owned by the various authors, illustrators and publishers who license us. The Ebookadabra application uses a number of sound effects courtesy of Soundbible.com and FreeSound.org. The copyright in the Ebookadabra platform is owned by Moondog Business Limited, a company registered in England and Wales with number 08873763 and registered offices at Lynton House, 7-12 Tavistock Square, London, WC1H 9LT, United Kingdom and is used by Moondog Books under license.

Ebookadabra is a mobile application which works on iPads and iPhones using iOS versions 8,9 & 10. We plan a release later in 2017 for the Android operating system.

Moondog Books has staff in the United Kingdom, France and Poland. As well as providing our own retail product we also help a number of large publishers deliver their content in mobile digital form. We are a group of digital developers, creatives and book content experts who believe that kids reading can be done better.

For more information please visit www.moondog books.com or contact us at contact@moondogbooks.com.
